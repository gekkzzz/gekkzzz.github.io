About

they / them · anti-social · infp

I'm from Wales, UK and diagnosed autistic af, which usually means my hyperfixations are changing all the time. One of the biggest ironies about me is that I absolutely hate flying in real life, but I will happily dedicate hours to piloting a plane in Microsoft Flight Simulator.

Gaming and media are a massive part of my everyday life. I play across an Xbox, PC, Switch 2, and PS5, jumping between everything from Oblivion and Stardew Valley to the Danganronpa games. I am also heavily into anime, specifically Jujutsu Kaisen and Jojo's Bizarre Adventure.

When I step away from the screens, my interests are a pretty even split between being active and being creative. I like to run, hit the gym, play guitar, and skateboard. I'm a big fan of F1, rugby, and football, with Swansea City AFC always in the mix.

To balance all of that out, I spend the rest of my downtime coding, reading non-fiction, and channelling my current hyperfixations into making things.
