About

they / them · anti-social · infp

I'm based in Wales, UK, and being proudly autistic means my hyperfixations are famously diverse and constantly changing. Case in point: I have a well-documented aversion to real-world flying; however, I will happily dedicate hours of my life to perfectly landing a commercial jet in Microsoft Flight Simulator.

When I'm not working, gaming and media take up a massive part of my day. You can usually find me platform-hopping across PC, Xbox, PS5, and Switch 2, playing everything from Oblivion and Stardew Valley to the Danganronpa games. I'm also a massive anime enthusiast; Jujutsu Kaisen and JoJo's Bizarre Adventure are particular favourites.

To ensure I actually see the sun occasionally, my offline life is an even split between staying active and getting creative. I run, hit the gym, play guitar, and skateboard. I'm also a dedicated follower of F1, rugby, and football, with a mandatory, unwavering loyalty to Swansea City AFC. I balance all of this out by reading non-fiction, and channelling my current fascinations into coding and building tangible projects.

Current Focus & Expertise

  • Full-Stack Development: Fully experienced on the front-end (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript); currently diving headfirst into Python, Linux, Git, and SQL to conquer the rest of the stack.
  • Data & Policy Analysis: Leveraging R and Python to make sense of complex data, legislation, and trends.
  • Digital Content: Video essayist, and editor.
  • Academic Background: BSc Econ in International Relations and Politics.

While I'm relatively new to the back-end side of development, my learning style is very much "jump in and figure out how to swim". I'll be documenting my progress and sharing what I build across this site, my GitHub, and my Substack.

My inbox is always open for collaborations, new perspectives, interesting projects, or just a good debate about JoJo plot holes.
