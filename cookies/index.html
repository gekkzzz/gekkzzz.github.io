What this site uses

This site does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. The only data sent to third parties is your IP address, used to show your local time and to choose a matching background animation for your current weather.

IP address lookup

Your IP address is sent to one of these services to detect your approximate location, timezone, and coordinates:

  • ipwho.is — ipwho.is
  • ipinfo.io — ipinfo.io

The returned coordinates (latitude and longitude) are then used for a weather lookup (see below). No data is stored by this site. Your IP is sent directly to these third-party services and is not logged or retained by gekkzzz.co.uk.

This lookup happens regardless of your cookie preference, as it is necessary for core site features (local time display and weather animation). No personal data is stored as a result.

Weather lookup

Once your approximate coordinates are known from the IP lookup, the site fetches current weather conditions from:

  • Open-Meteo — open-meteo.com — a free, open-source weather API

Only your latitude and longitude are sent. No IP address, name, or other personal data is included in this request. The weather code returned (e.g. "rain", "snow", "clear") is used solely to select a background animation that matches your local conditions. Open-Meteo requires no account and stores no personal data.

Local storage

This site stores the following items in your browser's localStorage:

  • gekkzzz-cookie-consent — your accept/reject preference for this notice
  • gekkzzz-theme — your chosen colour theme (dark, light, or auto)
  • gekkzzz-anim-enabled — whether the background animation is on or off

These are stored locally in your browser only and are never transmitted to any server.
