About Me

Being autistic means my interests are famously diverse; I view this as a distinct professional advantage rather than a hindrance. My approach to learning is very much to jump in at the deep end and figure out how to swim, which has led me to explore everything from software development to international policy.

Case in point: I have a well-documented aversion to real-world flying, yet I will happily dedicate hours of my time to perfectly landing a commercial jet in Microsoft Flight Simulator. When I am not virtually piloting aircraft, I channel these fascinations into building things. I document my ongoing progress and splashes across my GitHub, Substack, and Bluesky.
